--- paper_title: Advancing Renewable Electricity Consumption With Reinforcement Learning abstract: As the share of renewable energy sources in the present electric energy mix rises, their intermittence proves to be the biggest challenge to carbon free electricity generation. To address this challenge, we propose an electricity pricing agent, which sends price signals to the customers and contributes to shifting the customer demand to periods of high renewable energy generation. We propose an implementation of a pricing agent with a reinforcement learning approach where the environment is represented by the customers, the electricity generation utilities and the weather conditions. q1_track: Proposals primary_contact_author_name: Filip Tolovski primary_contact_author_email: tolovskifilip@gmail.com status: Accept (spotlight) primary_subject_area: Power generation and grids secondary_subject_areas: Data presentation and management; Industry author_names: Tolovski, Filip* author_emails: tolovskifilip@gmail.com* authors: Filip Tolovski (Fraunhofer Heinrich-Hertz-Institut) cmt_id: 50 zoom_id: 88216724805 session: Session2 slideslive_id: '38926224' id: 24 prev_paper_id: 23 next_paper_id: 25 rocketchat_channel: https://iclr.rocket.chat/channel/climatechange_poster_24 paper_path: "/papers/iclr2020/24/paper.pdf" slides_path: "/papers/iclr2020/24/slides.pdf" layout: paper workshop: iclr2020 ---
